R.S., c. D-1 Defence Production Act

Note marginale :2000, c. 31, s. 5

76.  Subparagraph 43(c)(vii) of the Defence Production Act is replaced by the following:

  • (vii) security assessments referred to in subsection 38(3) or 39.1(3); and

Defence Production Act

Clause 76: This amendment would correct a cross-reference. The security assessments in question are referred to in subsection 39.1(3), not section 39. It would therefore address an issue that was raised by the Standing Joint Committee for the Scrutiny of Regulations. It would also correct a terminological error in the English version. The security assessments are “referred to in” subsections 38(3) and 39.1(3), not “made under” those subsections.

Relevant portion of section 43:

43.  The Governor in Council may make regulations for carrying out the purposes and provisions of this Part, including regulations

  • ... 

  • (c) respecting registrations under section 38 and exemptions from registration under sections 39 and 39.1, including

    • ... 

    • (vii) security assessments made under subsection 38(3) or 39(3); and

R.S., c. E-3 Electoral Boundaries Readjustment Act

Note marginale :1993, c. 28, s. 78 (Sch. III, item 45); 1998, c. 15, s. 25

80.  Subsection 2(2) of the English version of the Electoral Boundaries Readjustment Act is replaced by the following:

  • Note marginale :Reference to a province or provinces

    (2) In this Act, a reference to a province or to provinces does not include Yukon, the Northwest Territories or Nunavut.

Electoral Boundaries Readjustment Act

Clause 80: This amendment would change “Yukon Territory” to “Yukon” in the English version because of the change of the name of that territory (see: Yukon Act, S.C. 2002, c. 7).

Existing text of subsection 2(2):

  • (2) In this Act, a reference to a province or to provinces does not include the Yukon Territory, the Northwest Territories or Nunavut.

81.  Subsection 13(1) of the Act is replaced by the following:

Note marginale :Return of Chief Statistician

  • 13. (1) As soon as possible after the completion of any decennial census, the Chief Statistician shall prepare and send to the Minister and to the Chief Electoral Officer a return certified by the Chief Statistician showing the population of Canada and of each of the provinces and the population of Canada by electoral districts and by dissemination areas as ascertained by that census.

Clause 81: This amendment would update terminology to reflect the fact that Statistics Canada no longer uses the phrase “enumeration area”. It has been replaced in Statistics Canada documents and the Standard Geographical Code by the phrase “dissemination area”.

Existing text of subsection 13(1):

  • 13.  (1) As soon as possible after the completion of any decennial census, the Chief Statistician shall prepare and send to the Minister and to the Chief Electoral Officer a return certified by the Chief Statistician showing the population of Canada and of each of the provinces and the population of Canada by electoral districts and by enumeration areas as ascertained by that census.

82.  The English version of the Act is amended by replacing “chairman” with “chairperson” in the following provisions:

  • (a) section 4;

  • (b) subsection 5(2);

  • (c) section 9;

  • (d) paragraph 13(2)(a); and

  • (e) subsection 20(1).

Clause 82: This amendment would modernize the language in the English version to conform to current standards of gender neutrality.

R.S., c. E-4 Electricity and Gas Inspection Act

Note marginale :2011, c. 21, s. 123(E)

83.  Subsection 26(5) of the English version of the Electricity and Gas Inspection Act is replaced by the following:

  • Note marginale :Restrictions

    (5) No person who is an accredited meter verifier or a seller of electricity, gas or meters or who is employed by or is the agent or mandatary of an accredited meter verifier or a seller of electricity, gas or meters may be appointed or designated under this section or act as a person so appointed or designated.

Electricity and Gas Inspection Act

Clause 83: This amendment would correct the English version by adding “gas or meters” so that the provision would also refer to a person who is employed by or is the agent or mandatary of a seller of gas or meters, making it consistent with the French version.

Existing text of subsection 26(5):

  • (5) No person who is an accredited meter verifier or a seller of electricity, gas or meters or who is employed by or is the agent or mandatary of an accredited meter verifier or a seller of electricity may be appointed or designated under this section or act as a person so appointed or designated.
