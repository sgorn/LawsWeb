Table of Private Acts (1867 to December 31, 2021)

The Table of Private Acts is a historical index showing all federal private Acts other than those dealing with divorce, that have been published in the Statutes of Canada since 1867. The Table also includes amendments and repeals of those Acts. Private Acts are added to the Table after December 31 of every year. Each Act is listed alphabetically under one of the following headings in accordance with its subject matter:

  1. BANKS
  2. BOARDS OF TRADE
  3. BRIDGES (see also RAILWAY BRIDGES AND TUNNELS)
  4. HARBOURS
  5. INSURANCE
  6. PATENTS
  7. PIPELINES
  8. RAILWAY BRIDGES AND TUNNELS
  9. RAILWAYS
  10. RELIGIOUS AND CHARITABLE ORGANIZATIONS
  11. TRUST AND LOAN COMPANIES
  12. MISCELLANEOUS
