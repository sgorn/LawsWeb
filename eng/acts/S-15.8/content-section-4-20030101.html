Marginal note:Transfer of property and obligations of the Corporation

  • 4 (1) All rights and property held by or in the name of or in trust for the Corporation and all obligations and liabilities of the Corporation are deemed to be rights, property, obligations and liabilities of Her Majesty.

  • Marginal note:Closing out affairs

    (2) The Minister may do and perform all acts and things necessary for or incidental to closing out the affairs of the Corporation.
