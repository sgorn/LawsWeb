5.012Â In addition to any other factor that it considers appropriate, the Commission of the Canadian Energy Regulator shall take into account the effects that its decision might have on navigation, including safety of navigation, when deciding whether to issue an authorization under paragraph 5(1)(b) in respect of a pipeline that passes in, on, over, under, through or across a navigable water.
