Marginal note:Documents

  • 5.37 (1) A holder of the leave required by paragraph 4.01(1)(d) and a holder of an authorization to construct or operate a pipeline issued under paragraph 5(1)(b), or the successor or assign of either holder, shall keep, in a form and manner determined by the Canadian Energy Regulator, any documents, including any records or books of account, that the Regulator requires and that contain information that is determined by the Regulator to be necessary for the administration of this Act and any regulations made under it.

  • Marginal note:Production and inspection

    (2) The holder of that leave and the holder of that authorization, or the successor or assign of either holder, shall produce those documents to the Canadian Energy Regulator, or make them available to the Regulator or its designated representative, for inspection or copying at a time and under conditions set by the Regulator.
