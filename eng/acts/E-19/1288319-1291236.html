2021, c. 1, s. 18

18 Schedule 3 to the Act is amended by adding, in alphabetical order, a reference to “CUKTCA” in column 1 and a corresponding reference to “Tables C.3 and C.4 of Annex 5-A to Annex 5 of the Protocol on Rules of Origin and Origin Procedures of CETA, as incorporated by reference in CUKTCA” in column 2.
