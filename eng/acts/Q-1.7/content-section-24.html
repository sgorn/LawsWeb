Marginal note:Disputes between Port Warden and parties, how decided

24Â Should any dispute arise between the Port Warden and any party interested, in any case where his presence has been required, either party may appeal to the board of directors of the Board of Trade and Industry of the Metropolitan Quebec, and it shall be the duty of the Secretary of the said Board of Trade and Industry, on a requisition being presented to him to that effect, to summon forthwith a meeting of the said board of directors, who, or not less than three of them, shall immediately investigate and report on the case submitted to them, and their determination or that of a majority of them, made in writing, shall be final and conclusive.
