Marginal note:For mining or milling purposes

126 The holder of a mineral claim or of any mill-site may obtain a grant to a water right of any unappropriated water for any mining or milling purposes under the Yukon Placer Mining Act, or under the provisions of the regulations for the disposal of water for power purposes, according to the purpose for which the water is to be used.

  • R.S., c. Y-4, s. 121
