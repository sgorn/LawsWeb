Marginal note:Yukon Territory and Northwest Territories

  • 5 (1) Where an employee is usually employed in the Yukon Territory or the Northwest Territories, the employee shall for the purposes of this Act be deemed to be usually employed in the Province of Alberta.

  • Marginal note:Nunavut

    (2) Where an employee is usually employed in Nunavut, the employee shall for the purposes of this Act be deemed to be usually employed in the Province of Alberta.
