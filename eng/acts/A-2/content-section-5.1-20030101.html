Marginal note:Restrictions and prohibitions for safety purposes

5.1 The Minister or any person authorized by the Minister may by notice prohibit or restrict the operation of aircraft on or over such areas or within such airspace, and either absolutely or subject to such exceptions or conditions as the Minister or person may specify, if, in the opinion of the Minister or person, the prohibition or restriction is necessary to ensure aviation safety.

  • R.S., 1985, c. 33 (1st Supp.), s. 1
