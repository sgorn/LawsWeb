Marginal note:Failure to achieve target

16 If the Minister concludes that Canada has not achieved its national greenhouse gas emissions target for a milestone year or for 2050, as the case may be, the Minister must, after consulting with the ministers referred to in section 12, include the following in the assessment report:

  • (a) the reasons why Canada failed to meet the target;

  • (b) a description of actions the Government of Canada is taking or will take to address the failure to achieve the target; and

  • (c) any other information that the Minister considers appropriate.
