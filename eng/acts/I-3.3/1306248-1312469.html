2021, c. 23, s. 29

  • 29 (1) Subsection 143.3(5) of the Act is amended by striking out “and” at the end of paragraph (c), by adding “and” at the end of paragraph (d) and by adding the following after paragraph (d):

    • (e) this section does not apply to prohibit the deduction of an amount under paragraph 110(1)(e).

  • (2) Subsection (1) comes into force or is deemed to have come into force on July 1, 2021.
