Marginal note:Distribution by trust

269Â For the purposes of this Part, where a trustee of a trust distributes property of the trust to one or more persons, the distribution of the property is deemed to be a supply of the property made by the trust at the place at which the property is delivered or made available to the persons and for consideration equal to the amount determined under the Income Tax Act to be the proceeds of disposition of the property.
