Marginal note:Exemption — Bridges Act

  • 6 (1) The Bridges Act does not apply to the bridge and related works.

  • Marginal note:Exemption — Service Fees Act

    (2) The Service Fees Act does not apply in respect of the tolls, fees and other charges fixed by regulations made under paragraph 12(b).
