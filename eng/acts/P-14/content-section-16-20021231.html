Marginal note:Authority a Public Service corporation

  • 16 (1) An Authority shall be deemed to be a Public Service corporation for the purposes of section 37 of the Public Service Superannuation Act.

  • Marginal note:Government Employees Compensation Act and Aeronautics Act

    (2) Officers and employees of an Authority shall be deemed to be persons employed in the public service of Canada for the purposes of the Government Employees Compensation Act and any regulations made pursuant to section 9 of the Aeronautics Act.
