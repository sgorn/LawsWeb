Marginal note:Definitions

2 In this Act,

apprentice pilot

apprenti-pilote

apprentice pilot means a person who is training to become a licensed pilot; (apprenti-pilote)

Authority

Administration

Authority means a Pilotage Authority established by section 3; (Administration)

Canadian waters

eaux canadiennes

Canadian waters means the territorial sea of Canada and all internal waters of Canada; (eaux canadiennes)

compulsory pilotage

pilotage obligatoire

compulsory pilotage means, in respect of a ship, the requirement that the ship be under the conduct of a licensed pilot or the holder of a pilotage certificate; (pilotage obligatoire)

compulsory pilotage area

zone de pilotage obligatoire

compulsory pilotage area means an area of water in which ships are subject to compulsory pilotage; (zone de pilotage obligatoire)

licence

brevet

licence means a licence issued by an Authority pursuant to section 22; (brevet)

licensed pilot

pilote breveté

licensed pilot means a person who holds a valid licence; (pilote breveté)

Minister

ministre

Minister means the Minister of Transport; (ministre)

pilot

pilote

pilot means any person not belonging to a ship who has the conduct thereof; (pilote)

pilotage certificate

certificat de pilotage

pilotage certificate means a certificate issued by an Authority pursuant to section 22; (certificat de pilotage)

ship

navire

ship includes any description of vessel or boat used or designed for use in navigation, without regard to method or lack of propulsion. (navire)
