SOR/2019-341

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to subsection 3(1.1)Footnote a of the Financial Administration ActFootnote b, amends Schedule I.1 to that Act by striking out the reference to the Minister of Industry in column II of that Schedule opposite the name of the Federal Economic Development Agency for Southern Ontario in column I of that Schedule and by substituting for that reference a reference to the Minister of Economic Development and Official Languages, effective November 20, 2019.
