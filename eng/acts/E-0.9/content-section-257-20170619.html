Marginal note:Replacement of terms and conditions

257 Every term and condition of employment of the employees in the bargaining unit that is continued in force, on the effective date, by section 107 of the Federal Public Sector Labour Relations Act and that is inconsistent with a term or condition of employment that is established as permitted by section 254 in respect of those employees is, on the effective date, replaced by that term or condition, as it is drafted to comply with section 255.
