Marginal note:General Manager

  • 16 (1) The directors shall appoint a General Manager of the Association.

  • Marginal note:Duties

    (2) The General Manager has, on behalf of the Board, the direction and management of the business of the Association with authority to act in all matters that are not by the by-laws or by resolution of the Board specifically reserved to be done by the Chairperson, the Board or the Executive Committee.

  • R.S., 1985, c. C-21, s. 16
