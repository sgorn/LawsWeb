Marginal note:First members

  • 10 (1) The first members of the Foundation are Robert Comtois, Victor Goldbloom, Fernand Legault, Robert Morin, Jean Roquet and Marcel Vincent, all of the City of Montreal, and John W. Pickersgill, Guy Roberge and Maxwell Yalden, all of the City of Ottawa.

  • Marginal note:First directors

    Footnote *(2) The persons mentioned in subsection (1) shall be the first directors of the Foundation until other directors are elected in their stead at a general meeting of the members to be held within three months after the coming into force of this section.

    • Return to footnote *[Note: Section 10 in force on assent December 18, 1981.]

  • Marginal note:Eligibility for election

    (3) The first directors are eligible for election at the meeting referred to in subsection (2).
