Marginal note:Definitions

66 The following definitions apply in sections 5 and 67 to 72.

authority

autorité

authority means

  • (a) a federal authority; and

  • (b) any other body that is set out in Schedule 3. (autorité)

project

projet

project means a physical activity that is carried out in relation to a physical work and is not a designated project. (projet)
