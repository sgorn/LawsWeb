    • Transitional

      • 47 (2) For greater certainty, the two year limitation period provided for in subsection 10(4) of the Act, as amended by subsection (1), only applies in respect of offences committed after the coming into force of that subsection.

AMENDMENTS NOT IN FORCE

  • — 2015, c. 2, s. 53(1)

      • 53 (1) The definitions livestock and sell in section 2 of the Act are replaced by the following:

        livestock

        livestock means any animals designated by regulation as livestock for the purposes of this Act; (animaux de ferme)

        sell

        sell includes agree to sell, offer for sale, expose for sale or have in possession for sale or distribute to one or more persons; (vente)
