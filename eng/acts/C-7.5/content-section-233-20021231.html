Marginal note:Appropriation

  • 233 (1) There may be paid out of the Consolidated Revenue Fund, for the purpose of making payments pursuant to this Part, amounts not exceeding, in the aggregate, the sum of two hundred and twenty-five million dollars.

  • Marginal note:Idem

    (2) Notwithstanding section 30 of the Financial Administration Act, any portion of the amount appropriated under this section may be expended in subsequent fiscal years.
