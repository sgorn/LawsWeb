Marginal note:Five-year review

  • *42 (1) During the fifth year after this Act is assented to, the Minister must review the provisions and operation of this Act in consultation with the Minister of Finance.

    • Return to footnote *[Note: Act assented to April 25, 1997.]

  • Marginal note:Tabling of report

    (2) The Minister must have a report on the review laid before each House of Parliament on any of the first 30 days on which it is sitting after the report is completed.
