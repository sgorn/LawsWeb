Marginal note:Not agent of Her Majesty

  • 17 (1) The Institute is not an agent of Her Majesty and the Chairperson and the other directors and the Executive Director, officers, employees and agents of the Institute are not part of the public service of Canada.

  • Marginal note:Part X not applicable

    (2) Part X of the Financial Administration Act does not apply to the Institute.
