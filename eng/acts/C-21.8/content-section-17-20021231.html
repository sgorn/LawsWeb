Marginal note:Not an agent

  • 17 (1) The Foundation is not an agent of Her Majesty.

  • Marginal note:Not part of public service of Canada

    (2) The Chairperson, directors, Executive Director, officers, employees and agents of the Foundation are not part of the public service of Canada.

  • Marginal note:Part not applicable

    (3) Part X of the Financial Administration Act does not apply to the Foundation.
