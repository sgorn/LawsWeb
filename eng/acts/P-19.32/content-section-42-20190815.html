Marginal note:Request to third-party state

42 No request under section 78 of the Extradition Act for the extradition or provisional arrest of a current or former preclearance officer may, without the consent of the Government of the United States, be made to a State or entity other than the United States with respect to an act or omission committed by the officer, if the Government of the United States has provided notice under paragraph 14 of Article X of the Agreement that it is exercising primary criminal jurisdiction over the act or omission.
