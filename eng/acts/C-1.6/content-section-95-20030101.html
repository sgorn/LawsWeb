Marginal note:Coming into force

  • Footnote *95 (1) Subject to this Act, this Act or any of its provisions, or any provision of an Act as enacted or amended by this Act, comes into force on a day or days to be fixed by order of the Governor in Council.

  • Marginal note:Condition

    (2) No order may be made under subsection (1) unless the Governor in Council is satisfied that the Government of the Republic of Chile has taken satisfactory steps to implement the Agreement.
