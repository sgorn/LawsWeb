2019, c. 29, s. 386

386 On the first day on which both section 675 of the Budget Implementation Act, 2018, No. 2 and section 384 of this Act are in force, section 4 of the Addition of Lands to Reserves and Reserve Creation Act is amended by adding the following after subsection (4):

  • Marginal note:Non-application of Statutory Instruments Act

    (5) The Statutory Instruments Act does not apply in respect of an order made under subsection (1).
