Marginal note:Review and report

  • 4 (1) Within five years after the day on which the report referred to in section 3 is tabled in Parliament, the Minister of Health must prepare a report on the state of palliative care in Canada, and cause the report to be laid before each House of Parliament on any of the first 15 days on which that House is sitting after the report is completed.

  • Marginal note:Publication of report

    (2) The Minister must post the report on the departmental Web site within 10 days after the day on which the report is tabled in Parliament.
