Marginal note:Coming into force

  • Footnote *60 (1) Subject to subsection (2), the provisions of this Act, or any provisions of an Act as enacted or amended by this Act, come into force on a day or days to be fixed by order of the Governor in Council.

  • Marginal note:Condition

    (2) No order may be made under subsection (1) unless the Governor in Council is satisfied that the Government of the Republic of Costa Rica has taken satisfactory steps to implement the Agreement.
