Marginal note:Definitions

2 The following definitions apply in this Act.

eligible contributor

participant admissible

eligible contributor means a person who is subject to requirements — set out in regulations made under any Act of Parliament — respecting emissions of greenhouse gas from industrial sources, other than a person who is a vehicle manufacturer. (participant admissible)

Fund

Fonds

Fund means the Greenhouse Gas Technology Investment Fund established in section 3. (Fonds)

greenhouse gas

gaz à effet de serre

greenhouse gas means any gas listed in Annex A to the Kyoto Protocol to the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change done at Kyoto on December 11, 1997, as amended from time to time, to the extent that the amendments are binding on Canada. (gaz à effet de serre)

Minister

ministre

Minister means the Minister of Natural Resources. (ministre)

vehicle

véhicule

vehicle means any vehicle that is capable of being driven or drawn on roads by any means other than muscular power exclusively, but does not include any vehicle designed to run exclusively on rails. (véhicule)
