Marginal note:Definitions

2 In this Act,

Agreement

Accord

Agreement means the Agreement on Internal Trade signed in 1994, as amended from time to time; (Accord)

Minister

ministre

Minister, in respect of any provision of this Act, means the member of the Queen’s Privy Council for Canada designated as the Minister for the purposes of that provision under section 8. (ministre)
