Marginal note:Meetings of Board

  • 16 (1) The Board shall meet at least twice in each year, with at least one such meeting at the head office of the Centre, and at such other times and places as the Chairperson deems necessary.

  • Marginal note:Chairperson to preside

    (2) The Chairperson shall preside at meetings of the Board.

  • Marginal note:Quorum

    (3) Seven governors, including at least five governors who are Canadian citizens, or more than seven governors, including a majority who are Canadian citizens, constitute a quorum of the Board.
