RELATED PROVISIONS

  • — 1989, c. 17, s. 16

    • Radio licences, etc.

      16 Radio licences, technical construction and operating certificates and radio operator certificates that were in force under the Radio Act immediately before this Act comes into force continue in force thereafter as if they had been issued in accordance with the Radio Act as amended by this Act.

  • — 1995, c. 1, s. 62(4)

    • Idem

      • 62 (4) Every reference to the Minister of Communications in any order, regulation or other instrument made under the Radiocommunication Act or the Telecommunications Act shall, unless the context otherwise requires, be read as a reference to the Minister of Industry.
