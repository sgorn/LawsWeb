2019, c. 13, s. 23

Marginal note:Replacement of “Committee”

23 The Act is amended by replacing “Committee” with “Agency” in the following provisions:

  • (a) subsections 6(2) and (4);

  • (b) subsection 12.1(3.5);

  • (c) subsection 17(2);

  • (d) subsection 19(3); and

  • (e) subsection 20(4).
