Marginal note:Return of documents

  • 155.89 (1) A document that is removed under paragraph 155.86(2)(h) must be returned to the person from whose custody it is taken within 21 days after it is taken or within any longer period that is directed by a judge of a superior court or agreed to by a person who is entitled to its return.

  • Marginal note:Notice of application for extension of time

    (2) An application to a judge for a direction under subsection (1) may only be made on notice to the person from whose custody the document is removed.
