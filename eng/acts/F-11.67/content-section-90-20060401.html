Marginal note:Definitions

90 The following definitions apply in this Part.

Institute

Institut

Institute means the First Nations Statistical Institute. (Institut)

other aboriginal group

autre groupe autochtone

other aboriginal group means an aboriginal group that was formerly a band under the Indian Act and that is a party to a treaty, land claim agreement or self-government agreement with Canada. (autre groupe autochtone)

respondent

intéressé

respondent means a person in respect of whom, or in respect of whose activities, a report or information is sought or provided under this Part. (intéressé)
