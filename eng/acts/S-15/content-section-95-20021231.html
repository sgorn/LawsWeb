Marginal note:Commissioner to provide name of importer

95 Where any person interested in the importation of goods into Canada requests the Commissioner to provide the person with the name of the importer of the goods, the Commissioner shall, except in prescribed circumstances, forthwith provide the person with the name of the importer.
