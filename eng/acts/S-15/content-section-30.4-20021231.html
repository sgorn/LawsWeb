Marginal note:Amount of subsidy

  • 30.4 (1) Subject to subsections (2) and (3), the amount of subsidy in relation to any goods shall be determined in the prescribed manner.

  • Marginal note:Where no prescribed manner

    (2) Where no manner of determining an amount of subsidy has been prescribed or, in the opinion of the Commissioner, sufficient information has not been provided or is not otherwise available to enable the determination of the amount of subsidy in the prescribed manner, the amount of subsidy shall, subject to subsection (3), be determined in such manner as the Minister may specify.

  • Marginal note:Exception

    (3) An amount of subsidy shall not include any amount that is attributable to a non-actionable subsidy.
