Marginal note:Definitions

91 The following definitions apply in this Part.

Investment Board

Office

Investment Board means the Canada Pension Plan Investment Board established by section 3 of the Canada Pension Plan Investment Board Act. (Office)

Minister

ministre

Minister means the Minister of Human Resources and Skills Development. (ministre)
