Marginal note:Definitions

2 The following definitions apply in this Act.

bargaining agent

agent négociateur

bargaining agent has the same meaning as in subsection 2(1) of the Public Service Labour Relations Act. (agent négociateur)

employer

employeur

employer has the same meaning as in subsection 2(1) of the Public Service Labour Relations Act. (employeur)

Minister

ministre

Minister means the Minister who is designated under section 3. (ministre)
