Marginal note:Remuneration of lay members

  • 6 (1) Each lay member shall be paid such remuneration as may be fixed by the Governor in Council.

  • Marginal note:Expenses of members

    (2) Each member of the Tribunal is entitled to be paid the travel allowances that a judge is entitled to be paid under the Judges Act.

  • Marginal note:Other benefits

    (3) Each lay member is deemed to be employed in the public service of Canada for the purposes of the Government Employees Compensation Act and any regulations made under section 9 of the Aeronautics Act.
