Marginal note:Suspension of interest and interest payments

9.4 During the period that begins on April 1, 2021 and ends on March 31, 2022,

  • (a) no interest is payable by a borrower on a student loan prescribed by regulations made under paragraph 15(1)(j); and

  • (b) no amount on account of interest in respect of a student loan prescribed by regulations made under paragraph 15(1)(j) is required to be paid by the borrower.

  • 2021, c. 7, s. 7
