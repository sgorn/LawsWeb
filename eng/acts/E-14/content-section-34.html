Marginal note:No erasure allowed in books

34Â No erasure shall be made in any books required to be kept in accordance with this Act by any person licensed under it, nor shall any leaf or leaves, or part of a leaf or leaves, be removed therefrom, and any obliterating of words or figures by any means whatever, other than by ruling through them with ink in such a manner as not to render the words or figures so ruled out incapable of being read, shall be deemed to be an erasure.
