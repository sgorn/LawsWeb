Marginal note:Assistance to quarantine officers

6 The person in charge of a conveyance boarded by a quarantine officer pursuant to section 5, and every person found therein, shall give the quarantine officer such assistance and information as may be prescribed or as the quarantine officer may, for the purpose of carrying out his duties and functions under this Act and the regulations, reasonably require the person to give.

  • R.S., c. 33(1st Supp.), s. 6
