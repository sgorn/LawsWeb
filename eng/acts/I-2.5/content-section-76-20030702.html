Marginal note:Definitions

76 The definitions in this section apply in this Division.

information

renseignements

information means security or criminal intelligence information and information that is obtained in confidence from a source in Canada, from the government of a foreign state, from an international organization of states or from an institution of either of them. (renseignements)

judge

juge

judge means the Chief Justice of the Federal Court or a judge of that Court designated by the Chief Justice. (juge)
