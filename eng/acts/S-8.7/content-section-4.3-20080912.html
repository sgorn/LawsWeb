Marginal note:Compliance if temporarily outside Canada

  • 4.3 (1) A sex offender who is outside Canada when they are required to report under section 4.1 shall report not later than 15 days after they return to Canada.

  • Marginal note:Canadian Forces

    (2) Subsection (1) does not apply to a sex offender who is required to report to a registration centre designated under the National Defence Act while they are outside Canada.
