Marginal note:Determination of amount of money compensation referrable to Tribunal de l’expropriation du Québec

127 Where compensation is payable wholly or partly in money pursuant to subsection 122(3) or (4) or paragraph 125(1)(e), and the parties cannot agree on the amount of such compensation, the amount shall be determined by the Tribunal de l’expropriation du Québec in accordance with the Expropriation Act (Quebec), unless the parties submit the matter to final and binding arbitration.
