Marginal note:Band may not be continued under Canada Business Corporations Act

  • 23 (1) Section 261 of the Canada Business Corporations Act does not apply to a band.

  • Marginal note:Canada Corporations Act

    (2) The Canada Corporations Act does not apply to a band.

  • Marginal note:Application of certain other Acts

    (3) Where provisions of an Act of Parliament specifically applicable to corporations, other than the Acts mentioned in subsections (1) and (2), would apply to a band, the Governor in Council may by order declare that such Act or any provision thereof does not apply to a band.
