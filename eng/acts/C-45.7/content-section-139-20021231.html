Marginal note:Land to be allocated for community services

  • 139 (1) A band shall allocate land necessary for community services provided by Quebec, its agents or mandataries, such as roads, schools, hospitals, police stations and other similar services.

  • Marginal note:Mode of allocation and fee

    (2) The allocation of land by a band pursuant to subsection (1) shall be effected by way of servitude, lease or similar contract, and for a fee not exceeding one dollar.
