Marginal note:Annual report

  • 42.1 (1) The Minister shall, as soon as possible after the end of each fiscal year, prepare and cause to be laid before Parliament a report on the administration and enforcement of the provisions of this Act relating to fish habitat protection and pollution prevention for that year.

  • Marginal note:Statistical summary

    (2) The annual report shall include a statistical summary of convictions under section 40 for that year.
