2019, c. 29, s. 384

Marginal note:2012, c. 19, par. 652(l)(E)

384 Section 44 of the First Nations Land Management Act is replaced by the following:

Marginal note:Non-application of Statutory Instruments Act

44 The Statutory Instruments Act does not apply in respect of a land code, First Nation laws or an order made under subsection 25.1(1).
