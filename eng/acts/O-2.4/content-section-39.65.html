Marginal note:Relief from minimum fine

39.65Â The court may impose a fine that is less than the minimum amount provided for in section 39.6 if it is satisfied, on the basis of evidence submitted to the court, that the minimum fine would cause undue financial hardship to the person who has been convicted of an offence. The court shall provide reasons if it imposes a fine that is less than the minimum amount.
