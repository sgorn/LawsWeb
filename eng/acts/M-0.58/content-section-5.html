Marginal note:Authorization to transfer shares of Marine Atlantic Inc.

  • 5 (1) The National Company is hereby authorized to transfer, and on the direction of the Minister shall transfer, to the Minister all the common shares of Marine Atlantic Inc. held by the National Company.

  • Marginal note:Provision not applicable

    (2) Subsection 101(1) of the Financial Administration Act does not apply in respect of a transfer of shares pursuant to subsection (1).
