Marginal note:Subsection 114(2) of the Canada Pension Plan does not apply

  • 43 (1) Subsection 114(2) of the Canada Pension Plan does not apply in respect of the amendments to that Act contained in sections 25 to 42.

  • Marginal note:Order in council

    Footnote *(2) Sections 25 to 42 come into force, in accordance with subsection 114(4) of the Canada Pension Plan, on a day or days to be fixed by order of the Governor in Council.
