Marginal note:Inspection

  • 22.1 (1) The Bank may require that the Superintendent of Financial Institutions perform, for a specified purpose, an inspection of any financial institution within the meaning of the Office of the Superintendent of Financial Institutions Act.

  • Marginal note:Costs

    (2) Where an inspection is made under subsection (1), such costs incurred in relation thereto as in the opinion of the Superintendent of Financial Institutions are extraordinary shall be borne by the Bank.
