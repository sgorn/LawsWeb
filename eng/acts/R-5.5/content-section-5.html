Marginal note:Applying Electoral Boundaries Readjustment Act

  • 5 (1) Upon the commencement of this Act, the Electoral Boundaries Readjustment Act shall be applied as if subsection 51(1) of the British North America Act, 1867, as amended by Part I of this Act, had been in force immediately following the decennial census of Canada taken in the year 1971, and electoral boundaries commissions shall be established and carry out their duties under the Electoral Boundaries Readjustment Act in all respects as though nothing had been done under that Act and no time had elapsed following the decennial census of Canada taken in the year 1971.

  • Marginal note:Presumption

    (2) Notwithstanding subsection (1), the certified return of the Chief Statistician of Canada referred to in section 11 of the Electoral Boundaries Readjustment Act and sent pursuant to that Act to the Secretary of State for Canada and the Representation Commissioner following the decennial census of Canada taken in the year 1971 shall be deemed to have been sent to, and to have been received on the day that this Act comes into force by, the Secretary of State of Canada and the Representation Commissioner for the purposes of applying that Act in accordance with subsection (1) of this section.
