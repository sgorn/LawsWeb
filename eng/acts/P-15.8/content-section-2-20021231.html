Marginal note:Definitions

  • 2 (1) The definitions in this subsection apply in this Act.

    collective agreement

    convention collective

    collective agreement means the collective agreement between the employer and the union that expired on July 31, 1997. (convention collective)

    employee

    employé

    employee means a person employed by the employer and bound by the collective agreement. (employé)

    employer

    employeur

    employer means the Canada Post Corporation. (employeur)

    mediator-arbitrator

    médiateur-arbitre

    mediator-arbitrator means the mediator-arbitrator appointed pursuant to subsection 8(1). (médiateur-arbitre)

    Minister

    ministre

    Minister means Minister of Labour. (ministre)

    union

    syndicat

    union means the Canadian Union of Postal Workers. (syndicat)

  • Marginal note:Words and expressions

    (2) Unless otherwise provided, words and expressions used in this Act have the same meaning as in Part I of the Canada Labour Code.
