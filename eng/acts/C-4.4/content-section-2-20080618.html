Marginal note:Definitions

2 The following definitions apply in this Act.

Auditor General

vérificateur général

Auditor General means the Auditor General of Canada appointed under subsection 3(1) of the Auditor General Act. (vérificateur général)

Board

Office

Board means the Canada Employment Insurance Financing Board established by subsection 3(1). (Office)

by-law

Version anglaise seulement

by-law means a by-law of the Board. (Version anglaise seulement)

Minister

ministre

Minister means the Minister of Human Resources and Skills Development. (ministre)
