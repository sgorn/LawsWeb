Marginal note:Effect of certificate or letters patent

39 On the day specified in

  • (a) the certificate of continuance issued under the Canada Business Corporations Act, or

  • (b) the letters patent issued under the Bank Act,

pursuant to an application referred to in subsection 38(1), the Canada Business Corporations Act or the Bank Act, as the case may be, applies and this Act ceases to apply to the body corporate continued under that Act.

  • 1991, c. 45, s. 39
  • 1994, c. 24, s. 34(F)
