Marginal note:When notice not required

  • 142 (1) A notice of a meeting of shareholders is not required to be sent to shareholders who were not registered on the records of the company or its transfer agent on the record date fixed or determined under subsection 140(3) or (4).

  • Marginal note:Effect of default

    (2) Failure to receive a notice of a meeting of shareholders does not deprive a shareholder of the right to vote at the meeting.
