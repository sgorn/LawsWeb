AMENDMENTS NOT IN FORCE

  • — 1992, c. 47, s. 84 (Sch., s. 9)

    • 1989, c. 7, s. 1

      9 Subsection 7(1) is amended by adding the word “and” at the end of paragraph (e) thereof, by striking out the word “and” at the end of paragraph (f) thereof and by repealing paragraph (g) thereof.

  • — 1992, c. 47, s. 84 (Sch., s. 10)

    • 1989, c. 7, s. 1

      10 Subsection 9(1) is repealed and the following substituted therefor:

      • Designation of inspectors

        • 9 (1) The Minister of Labour may designate any person to be an inspector for the purpose of section 10.

  • — 1992, c. 47, s. 84 (Sch., s. 11)

    • 1989, c. 7, s. 1

      11 Sections 13 and 14 are repealed and the following substituted therefor:

      • Service on corporations, etc.

        13 Service of a summons under the Criminal Code on an employer that is not an individual may be effected by delivery of the summons to the president, secretary or other executive officer of the employer, to the person appearing to have the control or management of any establishment of the employer, or to the employee or agent by whose act or omission the employer is alleged to have committed the offence.
