Marginal note:Where proceedings commenced under section 45 or 79

98 No application may be made under section 92 against a person

  • (a) against whom proceedings have been commenced under section 45, or

  • (b) against whom an order is sought under section 79

on the basis of the same or substantially the same facts as would be alleged in the proceedings under section 45 or 79, as the case may be.

  • R.S., 1985, c. 19 (2nd Supp.), s. 45
