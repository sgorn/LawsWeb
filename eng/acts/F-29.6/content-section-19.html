Marginal note:Presumption of advertising services for Canadian advertiser

19Â In a prosecution against a foreign publisher for an offence under section 3, proof that advertising content bears the name, trade-name or trademark of a Canadian advertiser or an address or a telephone number that appears to be that of a Canadian advertiser is proof, in the absence of evidence to the contrary, that advertising services were supplied by the foreign publisher to the Canadian advertiser or to a person acting on their behalf.
