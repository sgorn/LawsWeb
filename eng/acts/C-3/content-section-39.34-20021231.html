Marginal note:Determinations binding

  • 39.34 (1) The determinations of an assessor under sections 39.31, 39.32 and 39.33 are final and conclusive and, except for judicial review under the Federal Court Act, are not subject to appeal to or review by any court.

  • Marginal note:Rights before assessor in lieu of other rights

    (2) The rights of an offeree or a federal member institution under sections 39.24 to 39.33 are in lieu of any other right of action that the offeree or federal member institution or any person claiming through the offeree or federal member institution might otherwise have against the Corporation.
