Marginal note:Definitions

487.011 The following definitions apply in sections 487.012 to 487.017.

data

données

data has the same meaning as in subsection 342.1(2). (données )

document

document

document means any medium on which is recorded or marked anything that is capable of being read or understood by a person or a computer system or other device. (document)
