Marginal note:Mandatory solicitation

  • 166.04 (1) Subject to subsection 148(2) and subsection (2), the management of an association shall, concurrently with giving notice of a meeting of shareholders, send a form of proxy in prescribed form to each shareholder entitled to receive notice of the meeting.

  • Marginal note:Exception

    (2) If an association has fewer than fifteen shareholders, the management of the association is not required to send a form of proxy to the shareholders under subsection (1). For the purpose of this subsection, two or more joint shareholders are counted as one shareholder.
