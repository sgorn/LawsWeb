Marginal note:Dissolution instrument

  • 330 (1) Unless a court has made an order in accordance with subsection 331(1), the Minister may, if satisfied that the association has complied with subsection 329(4) and that all the circumstances so warrant, issue letters patent dissolving the association.

  • Marginal note:Association dissolved

    (2) An association in respect of which letters patent are issued under subsection (1) is dissolved and ceases to exist on the day stated in the letters patent.
