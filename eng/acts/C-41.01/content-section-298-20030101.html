Marginal note:Appointment of auditor

  • 298 (1) The members of an association shall, by ordinary resolution at the first meeting of members and at each succeeding annual meeting, appoint an auditor to hold office until the close of the next annual meeting.

  • Marginal note:Remuneration of auditor

    (2) The remuneration of an auditor may be fixed by ordinary resolution of the members but, if not so fixed, shall be fixed by the directors.
