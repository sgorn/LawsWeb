Marginal note:Definitions

  • 2 (1) In this Act,

    Board

    conseil

    Board means the Board of Directors of the Centre; (conseil)

    Centre

    Centre

    Centre means the International Centre for Human Rights and Democratic Development established by section 3; (Centre)

    Chairman

    président du conseil

    Chairman means the Chairman of the Board; (président du conseil)

    director

    administrateur

    director means a member of the Board; (administrateur)

    Minister

    ministre

    Minister means such member of the Queen’s Privy Council for Canada as may be designated by the Governor in Council as the Minister for the purposes of this Act; (ministre)

    President

    président du Centre

    President means the President of the Centre. (président du Centre)

  • Marginal note:International Bill of Human Rights

    (2) For the purposes of this Act, the International Bill of Human Rights means

    • (a) the Universal Declaration of Human Rights;

    • (b) the International Covenant on Civil and Political Rights;

    • (c) the Optional Protocol to the International Covenant on Civil and Political Rights; and

    • (d) the International Covenant on Economic, Social and Cultural Rights.
