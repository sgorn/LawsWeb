Marginal note:When forfeiture under s. 14(5)

  • 22 (1) An officer who retains currency or monetary instruments forfeited under subsection 14(5) shall without delay send them to the Minister of Public Works and Government Services.

  • Marginal note:When seizure or payment of a penalty

    (2) An officer who seizes currency or monetary instruments or is paid a penalty under subsection 18(2) shall without delay send the currency or monetary instruments or the penalty, as the case may be, to the Minister of Public Works and Government Services.
