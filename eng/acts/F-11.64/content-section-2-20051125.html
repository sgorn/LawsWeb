Marginal note:Definitions

  • 2 (1) The following definitions apply in this Act.

    first nation

    première nation

    first nation means a band as defined in subsection 2(1) of the Indian Act. (première nation)

    Minister

    ministre

    Minister means the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development. (ministre)

    reserve lands

    terres de réserve

    reserve lands means lands within a reserve as defined in subsection 2(1) of the Indian Act. (terres de réserve)

  • Marginal note:References to province

    (2) In this Act, references to a province, in relation to reserve lands, are to the province in which the reserve lands are situated.
