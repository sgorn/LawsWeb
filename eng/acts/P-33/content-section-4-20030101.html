Marginal note:President

  • 4 (1) The President is the chief executive officer of the Commission.

  • Marginal note:Acting President

    (2) The Governor in Council may authorize any commissioner to act as President if the President is absent or unable to act or if the office is vacant.

  • Marginal note:Quorum

    (3) A majority of the commissioners constitutes a quorum of the Commission.

  • Marginal note:Vacancy

    (4) A vacancy in the membership of the Commission does not impair the right of the remaining members to act.

  • Marginal note:Place of business

    (5) The Commission may sit at such times and places as it considers necessary or desirable for the proper conduct of its business.
