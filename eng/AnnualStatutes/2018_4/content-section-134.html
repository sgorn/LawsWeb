Marginal note:2009, c. 23

134 On the first day on which both subsection 19(2) of this Act is in force and section 352 of the Canada Not-for-profit Corporations Act has produced its effects, subsection 23(2) of the Naskapi and the Cree-Naskapi Commission Act is replaced by the following:

  • Marginal note:Canada Not-for-profit Corporations Act

    (2) The Canada Not-for-profit Corporations Act does not apply to the band.
