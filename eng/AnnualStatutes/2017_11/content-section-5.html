• 5 (1) Subsection 18(1) of the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act is replaced by the following:

    Marginal note:Examination by officer

    • 18 (1) Subject to the regulations, every person seeking to enter Canada must appear for an examination to determine whether that person has a right to enter Canada or is or may become authorized to enter and remain in Canada.

  • (2) Section 18 of the Act is amended by adding the following after subsection (2):

    • Marginal note:Powers of officer

      (3) Even though a person seeking to enter Canada is not, in accordance with regulations made under subsection 26(2), required to appear for an examination, an officer may require the person to do so.
