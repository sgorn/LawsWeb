4 The Wild Animal and Plant Protection and Regulation of International and Interprovincial Trade Act is amended by adding the following after section 7:

Marginal note:Cetacean: import and export

7.1 No person shall, except under and in accordance with a permit issued pursuant to subsection 10(1.1), import into Canada or export from Canada a living cetacean, including a whale, dolphin or porpoise, or sperm, a tissue culture or an embryo of a cetacean.
