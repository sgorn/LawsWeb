Marginal note:Chairperson and other members of former Board

101.Â The Chairperson and every other member of the former Board holding office immediately before the coming into force of this section continue to exercise their powers and perform their duties and functions as members of the new Board until the expiry or revocation of their appointment, despite subsection 10(4) of the Northwest Territories Waters Act, as amended by section 82.
