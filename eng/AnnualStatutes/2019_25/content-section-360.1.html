Marginal note:Section 83.3 of the Criminal Code

  • 360.1 (1) If, in accordance with section 83.32 of the Criminal Code, section 83.3 of that Act does not have effect on the day on which section 24 comes into force, then that section 24 is deemed never to have come into force and is repealed.
