Marginal note:$82,015,382,897 granted for 2018–19

2 There may be paid out of the Consolidated Revenue Fund a sum not exceeding in the aggregate $82,015,382,897 towards defraying the various charges and expenses of the federal public administration for the fiscal year ending March 31, 2019, not otherwise provided for, which is the total of the following amounts:

  • (a) $77,355,756,644, which is the total of the amounts of the items set out in Annex 2 to the Main Estimates for the fiscal year ending March 31, 2019 and set out in Schedule 1 to this Act less the total of the interim appropriations granted on account of those items by Appropriation Act No. 1, 2018–19; and

  • (b) $4,659,626,253, which is the total of the amounts of the items set out in Annex 2 to those Estimates and set out in Schedule 2 to this Act less the total of the interim appropriations granted on account of those items by Appropriation Act No. 1, 2018–19.
