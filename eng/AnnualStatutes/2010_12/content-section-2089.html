    • (c) if the approval relates to a federal credit union, whether the approval will affect its ability to be organized and carry on business on a cooperative basis in accordance with section 12.1.

    • (c) if the approval relates to a federal credit union, whether the approval will affect its ability to be organized and carry on business on a cooperative basis in accordance with section 12.1.
