68. Paragraph 60(x) of the Income Tax Act is replaced by the following:

  • Marginal note:Repayment under Department of Human Resources and Skills Development Act

    (x) the total of all amounts each of which is an amount paid by the taxpayer in the year as a repayment, under Part 5 of the Department of Human Resources and Skills Development Act, of an amount that was included because of subsection 146.1(7) in computing the taxpayer’s income for the year or a preceding taxation year.
