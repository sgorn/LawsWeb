Marginal note:Replacement of “Department of Human Resources Development Act”

81. The expression “Department of Human Resources Development Act” is replaced by the expression “Department of Human Resources and Skills Development Act” wherever it occurs in the following provisions of the Income Tax Act:

  • (a) subparagraph 110(1)(g)(i); and

  • (b) the definition “qualifying educational program” in subsection 118.6(1).
