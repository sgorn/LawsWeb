Marginal note:Child resuming attendance at school or university

68.Â If, before the coming into force of subsection 25(5) of the Canadian Forces Superannuation Act, as enacted by section 15 of this Act, payment of an allowance to a person ceased because, not being in full-time attendance at a school or university, that person was not a child within the meaning of paragraph 25(4)(b) of that Act, as that provision read before that coming into force, payment of the allowance to the person shall be resumed from the day that the person is a child within the meaning of paragraph 25(5)(b) of that Act, as enacted by section 15 of this Act, but in no case shall payment be made under this section from a day that is earlier than that coming into force.
