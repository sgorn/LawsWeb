An Act to amend the Copyright Act

SUMMARY

This enactment amends the Copyright Act to include, in the definition of “retransmitter”applicable to section 31 of that Act, retransmitters who currently benefit from the compulsory licence regime (such as cable distribution undertakings and direct-to-home satellite distribution undertakings), while excluding new media retransmitters whose retransmissions are lawful under the Broadcasting Act by reason only of the Canadian Radio-television and Telecommunications Commission’s Public Notice CRTC 1999-197.

The enactment also amends the conditions with which a retransmitter must comply in order to benefit from the compulsory licence regime, and enables the Governor in Council to make regulations prescribing additional conditions.
