Marginal note:Terminology changes — references and pre-February 14, 1995 handguns

56. The Act is amended by replacing the expression “referred to in subsection 12(6) (pre-February 14, 1995 handguns)” with the expression “referred to in subsection 12(6.1) (pre-December 1, 1998 handguns)” in the following provisions:

  • (a) section 20;

  • (b) subsection 54(3);

  • (c) subsection 67(3); and

  • (d) paragraph 120(2)(c).
