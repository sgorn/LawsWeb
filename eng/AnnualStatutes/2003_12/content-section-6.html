Marginal note:Reference to special duty areas

  • 6. (1) In the definition “special duty service” in subsection 3(1) of the Pension Act, as enacted by subsection 1(3) of this Act, the expression “special duty area designated under section 91.2” shall be read as including a special duty area designated by the Special Duty Area Pension Order made under subsection 91.1(1) of the Pension Act, as that subsection read immediately before the coming into force of this Act, until that order is repealed.

  • Marginal note:Reference to special duty areas

    (2) In the definition “special duty service” in subsection 32.1(2) of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police Superannuation Act, as enacted by section 4 of this Act, the reference to a special duty area designated under section 91.2 of the Pension Act shall be read as including a special duty area designated by the Special Duty Area Pension Order made under subsection 91.1(1) of the Pension Act, as that subsection read immediately before the coming into force of this Act, until that order is repealed.
