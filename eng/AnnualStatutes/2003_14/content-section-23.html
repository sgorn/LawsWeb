Marginal note:Bill C-36

23. If Bill C-36, introduced in the 2nd session of the 37th Parliament and entitled the Library and Archives of Canada Act (the “other Act”), receives royal assent, then, on the later of the coming into force of section 4 of the other Act and paragraph 13(c) of this Act, paragraph 13(c) of this Act is replaced by the following:

  • (c) personal information collected or obtained by the Library and Archives of Canada in the administration of the Library and Archives of Canada Act, or any predecessor enactment relating to the same subject-matter.
