Marginal note:2018, c. 12

186 On the first day on which both section 202 of the Budget Implementation Act, 2018, No. 1 and section 20 of the Retail Payment Activities Act, as enacted by section 177 of this Act, are in force, the definition professional trustee in section 2 of the Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation Act is amended by striking out “or” at the end of paragraph (e), by adding “or” at the end of paragraph (f) and by adding the following after paragraph (f):

  • (g) a payment service provider that is registered as those expressions are defined in section 2 of the Retail Payment Activities Act. (fiduciaire professionnel)
