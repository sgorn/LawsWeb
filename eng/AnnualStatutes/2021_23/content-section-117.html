• 117 (1) The definition adjustment day in section 58.1 of the Excise Act, 2001 is amended by striking out “or” at the end of paragraph (a.1) and by adding the following after that paragraph:

    • (a.2) April 20, 2021; or

  • (2) Subsection (1) is deemed to have come into force on April 20, 2021.
