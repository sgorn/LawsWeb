Marginal note:Replacement of “relevant registered trade-mark”

78 The Act is amended by replacing “relevant registered trade-mark” with “relevant protected mark” wherever it occurs in the following provisions:

  • (a) sections 51.05 and 51.06;

  • (b) sections 51.08 and 51.09;

  • (c) subsection 51.11(5); and

  • (d) section 51.12.
