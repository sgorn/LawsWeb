8 The Apprentice Loans Act is amended by adding the following after section 8.1:

Period — April 1, 2021 to March 31, 2022

Marginal note:Suspension of interest and interest payments

8.2 During the period that begins on April 1, 2021 and ends on March 31, 2022,

  • (a) no interest is payable by a borrower on an apprentice loan; and

  • (b) no amount on account of interest in respect of an apprentice loan is required to be paid by a borrower.
