Marginal note:Order in council

  • 372. (1) Subject to subsection (2), the provisions of this Act, other than subsections 297(2) to (4), (6) and (7) and sections 341 to 360, come into force on a day or days to be fixed by order of the Governor in Council.

  • Marginal note:Canada Pension Plan Investment Board Act

    (2) Subsections 317(1) and (2) come into force, in accordance with subsection 114(4) of the Canada Pension Plan, on days to be fixed by order of the Governor in Council.
